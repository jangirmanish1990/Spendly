Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal financial data that you voluntarily enter into the application. This includes expense amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We also collect basic account information such as your email address and authentication credentials necessary to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Specifically, we use your information to:

• Display your expense history and spending patterns
• Generate category breakdowns and financial summaries
• Maintain your account and authenticate your access
• Improve application functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

You retain ownership of all data you enter into Spendly. You may export or delete your data at any time through the application settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties for commercial purposes.

We may use trusted third-party service providers to host and maintain the application infrastructure. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, or unauthorized disclosure.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also export all your data in a portable format through the application settings.

To exercise these rights, please use the account settings within the application or contact us directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Your continued use of Spendly after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the new terms. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application.
